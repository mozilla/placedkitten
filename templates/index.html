Placedkitten

This is a Web Literacy tool that makes it easier to understand how servers and the HTTP protocol work.

This tool works by giving you URLs of images of kittens. You can then embed these URLs in web pages, give them to social networking websites, and share them with friends. This tool will then provide you with real-time information every time a device accesses your image. You can use this information to learn more about how the internet works.
